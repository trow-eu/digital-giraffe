About us

Digital Giraffe is a small game development company based in Groningen. Our consist of 5 very motivated game designers. We aspire to design top multiplatform games. The we are developing as a company is an educational game, but our team is very ambitios.

Our expertise

We make games. Our team is experienced with all aspects of game development, from concept to release.

  • Game design
  • Concept art
  • Gamification
  • 2d and 3d art
  • Development
  • Audio and Visual

We work on our original ideas and for clients.

Our games

Jelly Dream

Our current project in development. It's an educational 2d puzzle adventure game for 4 to 10 years old children. The game helps them learn new words and can be used during and outside school hours.
